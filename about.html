FORTUNE CONSULTANCY ABOUT SECTION

Fortune consultancy Website is an online platform where Malawian business organisations can register and customers can be able to order products online and communicate with the businesses and give feedback about how they feel about the products offered


fortune Consultancy also allows business organisation to have information about their customers and allow then to easily transact


On the navigation bar of the home page there is register when you click it you will be directed to a new page where you will register your business organisation,there is also order this is where customers can place orders to the registered business organisation,contact page gives you information how you can communicate with us
on the main body their is clients this is where customers can the name registered business organisation and there is also feedback,this is where customers can provide their feedback on the products offered by the registered business organisations

once a user register a message will be shown as an alert and the company's name will appear on the client page
the organisation account will be created where the will receive orders and feedback from customer

NOTE:feedback and order pages are for customers while register is for a business company that want to register on the website
